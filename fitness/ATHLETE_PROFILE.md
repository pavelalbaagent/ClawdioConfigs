# Athlete Profile (Pavel)

Last updated: 2026-03-05

## Goals

1. Primary: hypertrophy + fat loss.
2. Program cadence: 12-week build, then deload, then adapted next cycle.

## Schedule Preferences

1. Typical rest days: Wednesday and Sunday.
2. Schedule flexibility: yes, with rolling carry-forward for missed workouts.
3. Weekly structure: 4 main workouts + optional 5th workout.

## Split Preferences

1. Push/pull emphasis with arm specialization.
2. Prefer fewer exercises with more sets per movement.
3. Session cap target: 4-5 exercises.
4. Bench setup target: ideally only 2 days per week.
5. Time optimization via antagonistic supersets.

## Muscle Emphasis Preferences

1. Back focus: lats/width more than thickness.
2. Chest focus: incline/upper chest emphasis.
3. Biceps and triceps: high weekly volume with full head coverage across the week.

## Equipment and Constraints

1. Home setup: bench, dumbbells, barbell.
2. Plate inventory: 4x5 kg, 4x2.5 kg, 4x1.25 kg.
3. Practical goal: minimize bench/barbell setup and reduce frequent plate changes.

## Logging Preferences

1. Weight unit: kg.
2. Logging style: short conversational entries (for example: "12 reps, 10kg").
3. Weight representation policy:
   - Dumbbell default: per dumbbell (`each`).
   - Barbell default: total system load (`bb total`).
   - Optional overrides: `pair`, `bb side`.
