# Workout v4b (Arms Specialization, Low Variety)

Last updated: 2026-03-05

## Decision

You are right for your goal: lower exercise variety with higher repeat exposure is better for arm specialization.

## Why this is a good move

1. Better progression tracking when core arm exercises repeat weekly.
2. Lower setup and cognitive friction during sessions.
3. Higher quality effort close to failure on known movements.
4. Easier cycle-to-cycle variation (swap a few exercises after each 12-week block).

## Guardrails

1. Keep biceps and triceps volume high, but stable and recoverable.
2. Rotate exercises between cycles, not every week.
3. Keep enough back/chest work for joint health and force output.
4. Keep legs at minimum effective dose if arms are priority.
